Lower case

Lowercase is an extreme form of ambient minimalism in which very quiet sounds are amplified to extreme levels. Minimal artist Steve Roden popularized the movement with an album entitled Forms of Paper, in which he made recordings of himself handling paper in various ways.

In typography, all caps (short for "all capitals") refers to text or font in which all letters are capital letters; for example, THIS TEXT IS IN ALL CAPS. They are commonly seen in legal documents, the titles on book covers, in advertisements and in newspaper headlines.
