Ben 10: Alien Swarm is a 2009 American superhero film directed by Alex Winter and written by John Turman-James Krieg. It is based on the Cartoon Network's animated series Ben 10: Alien Force and is a standalone sequel to 2007 film Ben 10: Race Against Time. It stars Ryan Kelley, Galadriel Stineman, Nathan Keyes, Alyssa Diaz, Herbert Siguenza, and Barry Corbin.[2] The film follows the events which takes place between the second season's finale and the first episode of the third season of Ben 10: Alien Force. Ben 10: Alien Swarm held its world premiere in London on November 15, 2009,[3] and premiered on Cartoon Network on November 25, 2009,[4][5][6] where it was watched by 4.02 million viewers.[7] Plot
